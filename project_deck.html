Project: Funny Deck Game

A strategic card game, where how many slots you leave open for your cards matters

A deck-building video game where two players will face off while playing different creatures and attaching attacks and items to change their abilities. The game is inspired around a mix of Pokemon's battle mechanics and a more traditional TCG-type board strategy. Each game is a 2v2 and each player has a total of 4 lives. Developed in Python using Pygame, project took around a month.

A simple concept with tons of depth and strategy (working title "Deck Game": we never replaced it).

There are 4 kinds of cards! Creatures must be played first, then skills, items, and effects can be played to them in any of their 3 slots.

Cards are played, then battle insues!

The victor is the player that defeats 4 of their opponents creatures first!
