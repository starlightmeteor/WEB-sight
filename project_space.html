Project: Space Invaders

A fun little remake of a retro classic

A school project based on the 1978 "Space Invaders", developed in Pygame (Python) in two weeks. Scroll through the images below to see!

Opening screen is a simple pixel art crawl. Hovering over the High Score button displays the best of the best.

Early gameplay. Rudimentary Alien AI and shooting!

Finalized gameplay - player/alien formations and special flying saucer in action!

Includes barriers that protect you from aliens and protect aliens from you. 3 lives and you're out!
